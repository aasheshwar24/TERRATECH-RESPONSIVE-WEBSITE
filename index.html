How Hydroponic Farming Works

Hydroponics is a soilless farming technique where plants grow in a nutrient-rich water solution. This method ensures optimal growth by directly delivering essential nutrients to the plant roots.

Seed Germination

Seeds are placed in growing mediums like Rockwool or coconut coir to begin the sprouting process.

Nutrient Solution

Plants are submerged in a water-based solution enriched with essential minerals such as nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.

Light and Growth

LED grow lights or natural sunlight provide the necessary light spectrum for photosynthesis and plant development.

Harvesting

After a rapid growth cycle, plants are harvested fresh and pesticide-free, ready for consumption.
